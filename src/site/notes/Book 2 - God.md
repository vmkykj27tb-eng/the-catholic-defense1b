Book 2 is all about the existence, attributes, and abilities of God. In Book 2 I will dive deep into the complex questions regarding the Trinity, the question of his existence, and the overwhelming problems that come with his denial, including the knowledge of despicable evil in this world. This is a subject that is very complex, and I will be trying my best to present arguments based off of the Catechism of the Catholic Church plus some of the greatest Catholic and non Catholic but Christian thinkers, including C.S. Lewis.
