Modern Secularism, Atheism, Anti-Theism, and all anti-God sentiments share one thing: The belief in God is for the weak minded and for children. First off, to assume such things when some of the greatest scientists of all time who have made ideas like the modern scientific method, big bang, and modern genetics come from Catholic clergy. A non-Catholic example is James Clerk Maxwell, who was a Presbyterian. The Catechism of the Catholic Church states in CCC 31 that our abilities as humans make us capable of getting knowledge of the existence of a deeply personal God. The universe is not an accident, it is a testament to a supernatural, all powerful creator; God. 


If we actually DO look into the universe and space, true science destroys these slop Atheistic arguments through my personal favorite argument against Atheism, the Kalam Cosmological Argument. This is a foundational law of reality and reason that states that EVERYTHING that comes to exist MUST have a cause. The universe had a physical beginning at the start of time, space, and matter. This is a fact universally accepted by astrophysics. Because the universe cannot start on its own, its cause must transcend time and defy secular logic. By definition, this requires a non caused omnipotent being. Who is that you might ask? God and his will. 

Furthermore, the math of our universe show the absurdity of
random chance in the Fine-Tuning Argument. The constants of physics, such as the strength of gravity, the nuclear force, and cosmic expansion rates are so close to being off. If any of those values were altered by a 0.000000000000001x scale, the galaxies wouldn't of formed and life would be impossible. To claim this happened by coincidence (no such thing as a  coincidence by the way) is just wrong mathematically. As C.S. Lewis stated, if the universe was an accidental and unguided process, our minds would just be accidental bi-products of the accidental universe, meaning our capacity for thought would be untrustworthy. That would mean that if an Atheist uses that logic, their own logic against God just cannot work, as it would be untrustworthy. 

Finally, our own heart shows this cosmic logic. We posses morality, and an undeniable hunger for justice, truth, and purpose that our world cannot fill. No matter how rich, successful, and powerful you are, you will still be empty without God. This is also why many of the most atheistic countries on the planet of the highest depression and suicide rates. For example, South Korea, with one of the highest suicide rates in the world, has a 33 percent belief in God. South Korea is a highly developed, nice country. Compare that to the Philippines where 94 percent have an absolute belief in God, they have rate 12x less than a secular Japan. Now someone will use the Nordic countries as an exception, they are highly Atheist and very happy according to the UN stats. Those UN stats however measure stability, GDP, social nets, and paved roads as happiness measure, meaning they don't take in fulfillment. If you look past that, according to the European Health Information Survey, Finland and Swedens suffer from some of the highest rates of depressive disorders and clinical depression of young people in Europe. Their systems have provided a cage-you can have money, jobs, and free healthcare/college, but no purpose or fulfillment, which is why there are such high rates of drug rates and depression.
