Book 3 is when we really examine the start of Christianity, the reliability of the Gospels and New Testament, and what the Early Church really thought on key topics. Those same topics are what divide billions of Christians today, and as a strong Roman Catholic I examine the true beliefs and small details about the Church Fathers that everyone loves to claim. Book 3 attacks the criticism of the Resurrection and Crucifixion alike, and the claim that, "the Gospels were written far after Christ."
