Many uneducated people claim that Christ never existed so therefore Christianity is illogical. Many people actually argue that Christ never existed, and he's just a legend and a myth. This is incredibly false, however, as Christ existing is the most certain historical fact of all time. To deny His existence is just foolish and a blunder and historical ignorance. Secular, pagan, and Jewish historians who were alive during and immediately after the first century have a lot of writings that prove Christ's existence as a crucified, followed, and worshipped man.

This myth is destroyed when looking at the Roman records. In 115 AD, the pagan historian Tacitus documented Christ's crucifixion in his book The Annals (B 15, C 44). While writing about the emperor Nero's persecution of the early Church, he states that the founder of this church was a man called "Christus," and that he suffered an extreme penalty during the reign of emperor Tiberius at the hands of the Governor Pontius Pilate. Tacitus was a witness and was noted to hate Christianity, which means he had zero reason to lie about them. His legal records prove that people like Jesus, Pilate, and Tiberius existed. 

Jewish historical records of the time provide another record of Christ's life. The loved Jewish historian Flavius Josephus, who wrote for the Roman Empire in the book The Antiquities of the Jews (B18 C 3), records the existence of Jesus. Josephus details that Jesus was a wise teacher, who did startling deeds, and a teacher of those who receive truth, and that Pilate condemned Him to execution. Basically all accredited historians whether Atheist, Agnostic, or Christian accept the existence of Jesus. As The Catechism of the Catholic Church states in CCC 515, the gospels aren't fake, they are records of written by men who showed the fidelity of Jesus, who lived among them and died for their salvation. 

You cannot debate the teachings of Christ if you are hiding behind Him never existed. The evidence proves otherwise for his birth, life, execution, and how His movement started. He has more manuscripts written closer for His existence then Caesar or Alexander the Great. Frank Sheed (He's a great apologist) noted how the mind can't remain neutral on a topic when provided with evidence and truth. Salvation isn't based off of a false man, it's built off the most documented man in history. He was recorded by those who hated Him, and started the Catholic Church.
